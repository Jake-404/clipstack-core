# Privacy — template

> **Template, not advice.** Have counsel review before you ship this to a paying customer. Last reviewed by counsel: *(none)*.

This document explains what data the Clipstack platform collects, why, where it lives, and your rights.

## 1. What we collect

### From your workspace

- **Account data** — name, email, role, billing details where applicable.
- **Workspace artifacts** — brand kits, voice corpora, lessons, drafts, approvals, agent configurations, audit log.
- **Channel-connected data** — when you connect a CRM, analytics, ads, CMS, or other adapter, the platform reads what's needed for the configured workflow. Read-only by default; write operations are explicit per integration.
- **Email ingestion** (if connected) — Gmail / Outlook threads ingested for the Inbox surface. **Read-only.** Clipstack never replies on your behalf.

### Generated by you using the platform

- **Drafts and shipped content.** You own these (see [IP-Ownership.md](./IP-Ownership.md)). We store them so the platform's institutional-memory layer functions.
- **Approval rationales.** When you deny a draft, the rationale you provide is captured as a lesson. This stays in your workspace; it never trains shared models.
- **Performance data.** Where you've connected analytics, post-publication metrics flow back into the platform to improve future suggestions for *your* workspace.

### Telemetry

- **Operational metrics** — request counts, latency, error rates. Aggregated, no PII.
- **Cost telemetry** — token spend, model calls per workflow. Used for billing accuracy and platform tuning.
- **Crash reports** — stack traces with personal data redacted. Stored 30 days.

We do not sell telemetry. We do not use it to retarget you advertising.

## 2. What we don't collect

- **No third-party trackers** on the application. (Marketing site is separate — see [§7](#7-the-marketing-site).)
- **No model-training pipeline on customer data by default.** Per [IP-Ownership.md](./IP-Ownership.md), workspace artifacts never train shared models unless you explicitly opt into a shared-improvement program (with disclosed value exchange).
- **No reading of your channels** beyond what an active workflow needs. We don't scan your CRM for marketing data.

## 3. Where data lives

- **Hosted SaaS** — primary region declared at signup (US-East / EU-West / AP-Southeast). Cross-region replication for DR (RPO 15 min) within the same regulatory zone. We do not move data across zones without an explicit cross-zone agreement.
- **Self-hosted** — wherever you deploy. We don't see it.

Object storage (uploaded assets, brand kits) replicates within the same zone. Vector store (Qdrant — embeddings of lessons + content) lives in the same zone.

## 4. How long we keep it

| Category | Retention |
|---|---|
| Account data | While the account is active + 90 days post-cancellation, then anonymised |
| Workspace artifacts | While the account is active; export available on cancellation; deleted 90 days post-cancellation unless you've requested earlier deletion |
| Audit log | 7 years (compliance baseline; contractual extensions on request) |
| Telemetry (aggregated) | 13 months rolling |
| Crash reports | 30 days |
| Email ingest cache | 30 days rolling — we don't accumulate inbox history |

Earlier deletion on request — see §6.

## 6. Your rights

- **Access** — request a copy of all data we hold about you. We respond within 30 days.
- **Correction** — fix anything inaccurate in your account.
- **Deletion** — request earlier-than-default deletion. We honour within 30 days of confirmation, except where law requires retention (audit log, billing records).
- **Portability** — receive your workspace artifacts in standard formats (JSON for structured data, markdown for content, CSV for tabular). Available any time from your settings or on cancellation.
- **Restriction / objection** — pause specific processing types where applicable under GDPR / CCPA / your local law.

To exercise rights: *(privacy contact email TBD)*. If you're in the EU we have a data-protection contact at *(DPO email TBD)*.
